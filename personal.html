Projects

Whatsapp Chat Analysis Using Machine Learning

This project focuses on leveraging machine learning techniques to analyze individual WhatsApp chats, aiming to uncover and understand various patterns within the conversations. By meticulously examining the chat data, the system identifies and highlights key aspects such as message frequency, commonly used words, and the overall sentiment of the messages, whether they are positive, negative, or neutral.

Water Pollution In Villages

The main goal of a water pollution project is to identify and mitigate the sources and impacts of water pollution to ensure clean, safe, and sustainable water resources. This involves assessing water quality, identifying pollution sources, implementing pollution reduction strategies, raising public awareness, protecting aquatic ecosystems, promoting sustainable practices, and advocating for effective policies and regulations. By addressing these key areas, the project aims to safeguard water resources for drinking, agriculture, recreation, and ecosystem health.
